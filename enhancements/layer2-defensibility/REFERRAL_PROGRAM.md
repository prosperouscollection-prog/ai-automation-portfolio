# Genesis AI Systems Referral Program

## Refer a Business and Earn $50-$500

If you know another business that could benefit from AI lead capture, voice automation, reporting, or workflow automation, Genesis AI Systems will thank you for the introduction.

## How It Works

1. Refer a business to Genesis AI Systems.
2. We speak with them and confirm the project fit.
3. If they become a paying client, you earn a referral reward.

## Referral Reward

Genesis AI Systems pays `10%` of the first setup fee, typically ranging from `+$50` to `+$500` depending on the project size.

## Great Referral Fits

- dental offices

- real estate agents
- any service business losing leads due to slow response times

## Why Clients Refer

- they look helpful to other business owners
- they get rewarded for something they already believe in
- the process is simple and low-friction
