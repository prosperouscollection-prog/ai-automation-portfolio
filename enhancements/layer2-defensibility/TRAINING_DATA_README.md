# Vertical Training Data Strategy

## Overview

The `training_data/` folder contains starter JSONL templates for high-value verticals Genesis AI Systems can serve repeatedly:

- dental
- HVAC
- salon
- restaurant
- real estate

Each file includes `25` examples in OpenAI fine-tuning format so you can sell custom tone and knowledge alignment before you have a large client-owned dataset.

## How to Collect Real Client Data

Start with:

- FAQs from the website
- phone call transcripts
- chat logs
- email replies
- team-written scripts
- objections heard by staff

Then convert those into:

- common questions
- common edge cases
- tone examples
- escalation situations

## How to Grow from 25 to 100+ Examples

1. Launch with the vertical starter template.
2. Capture real interactions for 30 days.
3. Label the best and worst responses.
4. Add corrections for missed edge cases.
5. Expand by service line, objection type, and urgency level.
6. Retrain when the dataset meaningfully improves.

## Pricing Guidance

Charge at least `+$2,000` for custom fine-tuning work per vertical, and more when:

- the client has multiple locations
- the client wants strong brand voice matching
- the client provides a rich dataset
- the fine-tuned model will power voice, chat, and email together

## Positioning

Do not sell this as "we trained a model."
Sell it as:

- brand-consistent AI
- less generic responses
- better handling of niche business details
- a growing knowledge asset the client benefits from over time
