# Genesis AI Systems Uptime SLA

Genesis AI Systems guarantees `99.5%` uptime for the covered automation systems during each monthly billing cycle.

## Covered Systems

- hosted chat widgets
- workflow automations
- monitoring-backed webhook systems
- reporting systems under active retainer

## Exclusions

This SLA does not apply to outages caused by:

- client-side credential changes
- third-party platform outages outside Genesis AI Systems control
- DNS, domain, or hosting changes made by the client
- force majeure events

## Credit Policy

If Genesis AI Systems misses the `99.5%` uptime guarantee in a given month, the client receives a `10%` credit on the next invoice.

## Client Obligations

To receive SLA coverage, the client must:

- keep required credentials active
- notify Genesis AI Systems of infrastructure changes
- maintain any third-party subscriptions required for the system to operate
