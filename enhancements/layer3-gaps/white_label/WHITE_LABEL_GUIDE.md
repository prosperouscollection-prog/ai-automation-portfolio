# White Label Guide

## Overview

Genesis AI Systems can be resold by agencies that want to offer AI automation without building the systems themselves.

## What Can Be Rebranded

- widget colors
- client-facing agent name
- email sender name and domain
- reporting logos
- dashboard branding

## What Cannot Be Changed

- core architecture
- compliance guardrails
- security standards
- Genesis AI Systems internal operating methods

## Pricing Model

- Genesis AI Systems charges the reseller roughly `60%` of retail
- the reseller marks up to `100%` based on its market and services

## Support Model

- Genesis AI Systems supports the reseller
- the reseller supports its end clients

## Contract Addendum

Always include a reseller-specific addendum covering:

- support boundaries
- white label permissions
- IP restrictions
- termination rights
