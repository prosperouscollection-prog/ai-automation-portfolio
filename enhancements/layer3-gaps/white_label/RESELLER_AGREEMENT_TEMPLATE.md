# Reseller Agreement Template

## Parties

This agreement is between Genesis AI Systems and [Reseller Name].

## Scope

Genesis AI Systems will provide white label AI systems for resale by the Reseller under the agreed commercial terms.

## IP Protection

- Genesis AI Systems retains ownership of its internal architecture, methods, prompts, templates, and automation frameworks.
- Reseller receives a limited right to market and resell the covered systems.

## Non-Compete / Non-Circumvention

- Reseller may not hire or contract directly with Genesis AI Systems end clients introduced through the white label relationship.
- Reseller may not present Genesis AI Systems internal systems as its own proprietary IP.

## Revenue Terms

- reseller pricing, payment timing, and markup rights should be listed in each order form

## Termination

Either party may terminate with written notice under the agreed commercial terms, subject to any active client commitments already in progress.
