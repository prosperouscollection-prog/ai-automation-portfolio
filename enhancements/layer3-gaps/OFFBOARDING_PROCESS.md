# Client Offboarding Process

## Purpose

Offboarding should feel organized, professional, and calm. A clean offboarding process protects Genesis AI Systems legally, preserves reputation, and creates a chance to win the client back later.

## 30-Day Notice Requirement

Require written cancellation notice at least `30 days` before the final service date.

Recommended wording:

> Client may cancel recurring services with 30 days written notice. Services remain active through the notice period and all fees due during that period remain payable.

## Data Export Checklist

Before final deactivation, prepare:

- Google Sheets export
- workflow JSON exports
- prompt or FAQ documents the client owns
- reporting PDFs already generated for the client
- any client-supplied assets or branded templates

## What Genesis AI Systems Retains

Genesis AI Systems retains:

- anonymized performance learnings
- generalized prompt patterns
- internal workflow architecture
- non-client-specific process improvements

## System Deactivation Checklist

1. confirm final service date in writing
2. stop automated triggers
3. remove client notification recipients
4. archive credentials securely
5. export client-owned data
6. confirm report delivery is complete
7. revoke access where appropriate

Before that date, we will:

- prepare your data export
- complete final reporting
- deactivate the system after the notice period

If you have any questions during offboarding, reply here and we’ll help.

[Your Name]

## Re-Engagement Sequence

### Day 30

Check in with:

- a brief status question
- one result reminder
- an offer to restart quickly

### Day 60

Send one useful insight or industry trend tied to their use case.

### Day 90

Offer a reactivation call with reduced restart friction.

## Data Deletion Timeline

Delete sensitive client data within `90 days` of the final service date unless:

- the contract requires a different retention period
- there is an active billing dispute
- the client requests an earlier export and deletion sequence

## Dispute Handling

- keep all communication in writing
- provide the signed contract and invoice history
- do not argue emotionally
- complete only the obligations that remain active under the contract

## Offboarding Upside

Always ask for:

- a testimonial if the relationship ended positively
- permission to use results in anonymized case studies
- a referral if the client no longer needs the system personally
