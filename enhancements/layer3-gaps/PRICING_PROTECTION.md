# Pricing Protection

## Why This Matters

AI service businesses lose margin quietly when they fail to cap usage in the contract. Genesis AI Systems should always define what is included and what triggers overages.

## Contract Language to Use

Add clauses that define:

- included monthly usage
- what counts as usage
- overage billing method
- review rights if usage spikes dramatically

Example:

> Monthly service includes up to [X] API calls, [Y] tokens, [Z] voice minutes, and [N] automated emails. Usage above these limits may be billed at the rates outlined in the service order.

## Best Practices

- keep the included limits generous enough to feel easy
- make overage rates simple
- review plan fit before the client feels surprised
- never subsidize runaway usage silently
