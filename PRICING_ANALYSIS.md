## Pricing Strategy Analysis

### ✅ **Strengths**

1. **Starter Tier ($500 + $150/mo)** — **VERY COMPETITIVE**
   - Includes setup, AI system, monthly support
   - Typical DIY costs: $300-600/mo + your time
   - **Sweet spot for SMBs** who want done-for-you

2. **Transparent & Simple**
   - No per-minute billing mystery
   - No hidden fees
   - Clear value progression between tiers

3. **Done-For-You Model**
   - Competitors charge raw API costs ($500-2,000/mo for voice alone)
   - You handle setup, training, monitoring
   - This is worth $2,000-5,000/mo in salary costs alone

4. **Bundled Services**
   - Growth tier includes: phone AI + lead capture + management
   - Individual equivalents: $3,000-6,000/mo minimum
   - **$650/mo is a steal** for this bundle

### ⚠️ **Opportunities**

1. **Growth Tier Gap** ($150 → $650 is 4.3x jump)
   - Consider intermediate tier: "Pro" at $300-400/mo?
   - Would capture price-sensitive customers who need more than Starter

2. **Price Anchoring**
   - Full Stack at $7,600/mo might feel expensive without context
   - Add comparison: "Equivalent agency service: $15,000-25,000/mo"
   - Highlights your 60-70% discount

3. **Value Message Missing**
   - Don't say "$150/month"
   - Say "**Save $500+/mo vs building this yourself** ($150/month our cost)"
   - ROI language is more powerful
