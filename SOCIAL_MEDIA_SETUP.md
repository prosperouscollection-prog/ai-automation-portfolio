## Posting Frequency & Best Practices

- **LinkedIn:**
  - Mix of personal journey, AI explainers, client stories, and calls to action.
  - Weekly publishing at minimum; more frequent for launches, new features, and notable results.
  - CTA in every post (demo link, DM, comment invite).

- **Instagram:**
  - Feed: Case studies, product features, agency milestones.
  - Stories: Live bots in action, 24-hour AI response, Q&A features.
  - Link in bio = main website or current campaign/demo.
  - Include the hashtag `#GenesisAISystems` in every post.

- **Community Engagement:**
  - Respond to all comments and DMs within 24 hours.
  - Like and share local business and Detroit tech content to boost community strength.
  - Cross-promote launches and case studies on both platforms.

- **Example Call-to-Actions:**
  - "Book a free AI demo call today."
  - "DM for portfolio access."
  - "Click link in bio for a live tour."
