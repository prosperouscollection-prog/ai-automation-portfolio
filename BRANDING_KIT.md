## Visual Direction

The look should feel:

- premium
- modern
- technical but approachable
- confident without being flashy
- trustworthy for small business owners

Avoid:

- generic startup gradients with purple
- playful or cartoonish graphics
- cluttered dashboards in thumbnails
- overly “AI sci-fi” visuals

- `Inter`

- body text

- button labels

## Design Rules

### Buttons

- Primary CTA: electric blue fill with white text
- Secondary CTA: transparent dark panel with light border
- Rounded pill style

### Cards

- dark panel background
- subtle light border
- soft shadow
- rounded corners

### Layout

- lots of breathing room
- strong section spacing
- concise copy
- metrics and outcomes featured prominently

## Messaging Framework

### What To Lead With

Always lead with:

1. the business problem
2. the automated outcome
3. the speed or ROI improvement

### What Not To Lead With

Avoid opening with:

- model names
- API details
- technical architecture
- “I’m passionate about AI”

## Case Study Metric Style

When showing outcomes, use short metric phrases like:

- `4 hrs -> 10 sec`
- `12-15 calls/week recovered`
- `60 min -> 3 min`
- `3-5 hours/week saved`

Keep metrics believable and operational, not hype-heavy.
