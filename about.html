The founder story

Hi. I am Trendell Fordham.

I grew up in Detroit watching local businesses work harder than anyone else and still lose to bigger companies with more money and more technology.

Not because they offered worse service. Not because they cared less about their customers.

But because when someone called at 9pm nobody answered. Because a lead came in at midnight and sat there until morning. Because the owner was too busy doing everything else to follow up before a competitor did.

That always bothered me.

So I spent years learning everything I could about artificial intelligence and automation. I built system after system testing each one on real businesses to see what actually worked.

Then I made a rule for myself: every system I sell to a client has to run on my own business first. Genesis AI Systems uses all 14 of our own products. We are our own customer zero. If it does not work for us we do not sell it to you.

Today we help local businesses across Detroit and beyond answer their phones, capture their leads, respond to their customers, and grow their revenue — all without the owner working one extra hour.

I am based right here in Detroit. I answer my own calls. I personally oversee every system we build. And I am just getting started.
