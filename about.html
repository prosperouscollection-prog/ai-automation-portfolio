Why I built this

Hi. I am Trendell Fordham.

I grew up watching Detroit businesses work harder than anyone and still lose customers to bigger companies with more resources.

Not because they offered worse service. Not because they cared less.

But because a customer called at 9pm and nobody answered. Because a lead came in at midnight and sat there until morning. Because the owner was too busy doing everything else to follow up in time.

That bothered me.

So I learned everything I could about AI and automation. I spent months building systems that could do the repetitive work. Answering phones. Following up on leads. Responding to questions. Booking appointments.

Then I tested every single system on my own business first.

If it did not work for Genesis AI Systems I did not sell it to anyone else.

Today every system I sell has been running on my own agency. You are not a test case. You are getting something already proven.

I am based right here in Detroit. I answer my own phone. I personally oversee every system I build. And I am not done building.
