Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Genesis AI Systems takes your privacy seriously. We aim for full transparency and a clear explanation of your rights and our obligations. This Privacy Policy describes what we collect, how we use it, and your choices. If you have a question, contact us any time at info@genesisai.systems.

2. Information We Collect

  • Name, email, phone, and business name: Shared when you book a call, try a demo, or contact us.
  • Usage data and demo interactions: Simple analytics (like page views and demo feature use) to improve our service.
  • Call recordings with consent: If you call our AI agent Riley, we may record calls—with your consent—for quality and training.

3. How We Use Information

  • To respond to your inquiries, demo requests, or follow-up questions.
  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • We never sell your data to third parties.

4. AI Systems and Data

  • OpenAI: Powers lead scoring and some internal data analysis (form and chat data processed).
  • Vapi: Manages calls to Riley, our AI voice agent (handles your phone call data).
  • Claude: Handles queries in our website chat widget.
  • Google Sheets: Securely stores information using encrypted Google infrastructure.

5. Data Retention

  • Lead data (including name, contact, business info) is stored for 2 years.
  • Call recordings are kept for 90 days, then deleted automatically.
  • We will delete your data on written request. Just email us at info@genesisai.systems.

6. Your Rights (CCPA compliant)

  • Right to know: You can ask us what data we have collected about you.
  • Right to delete: You can request your information be deleted at any time.
  • Right to opt out: You can opt out of data sharing and processing. (We do not sell your data.)

To exercise your rights, simply email info@genesisai.systems.

7. Cookies

  • We use only minimal analytics cookies (no targeted advertising).
  • These help us understand site usage and improve performance.

You can disable cookies in your own browser settings. Most browsers let you block, allow, or delete cookies at any time. Some features may work less optimally if cookies are disabled, but you may still use our site.

8. AI Transparency (Colorado AI Act)

  • AI is used for lead scoring, to power the Riley voice agent, and run our chat widget.
  • All major service decisions impacting you are reviewed by a human.
  • We clearly tell you when you are interacting with AI (on calls or chat).
