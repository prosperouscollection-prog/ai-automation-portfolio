Privacy Policy

Effective March 29, 2026

1. The short version

We collect the details you share with us so we can answer your questions, follow up on your request, and do the work you asked us to do. We do not sell your information.

2. What we collect

  • Your name, business name, phone number, and email address when you fill out a form, book a call, or contact us.
  • The business details you share with us so we can recommend the right service.
  • Basic site use details, like which pages people visit, so we can improve the site.
  • Call details if you call our business line and the phone system keeps a record of that call.

3. Why we collect it

  • To respond to your message or booking request.
  • To set up and care for the service you bought.
  • To send updates, reports, and helpful follow-up notes.
  • To keep the business running well and fix problems faster.

4. Who may see it

Your information may be handled by trusted companies we use for phone calls, email, scheduling, payment, and spreadsheets. They help us run the business, but they do not own your information.

5. How long we keep it

We keep your details for as long as we need them to do the work, answer questions, and keep clear business records. If you want your information removed, email us and we will review that request quickly.

6. Your choices

  • You can ask what information we have about you.
  • You can ask us to correct something that is wrong.
  • You can ask us to remove your information when we no longer need it.
  • You can ask us not to send you follow-up emails.
