# RAG Knowledge Base Chatbot

An AI chatbot grounded in your business FAQ so it can answer repetitive customer questions accurately and escalate safely when needed.

## Problem It Solves

Staff lose hours every week answering the same customer questions, which slows response times and pulls attention away from higher-value work.

## Target Client

Any business with repetitive customer questions.

## Tech Stack

- Claude API
- Custom FAQ document
- Claude Projects

## Configuration and Deployment

1. Gather the client's FAQ, policies, service details, and approved support answers.
2. Organize the content into a structured knowledge document.
3. Configure the Claude project with retrieval instructions, tone guidelines, and escalation rules.
4. Test common questions to confirm the chatbot stays grounded in the provided material.
5. Deploy the chatbot in the client's chosen interface and update the source document as the business changes.

## Demo Instructions

1. Ask, `Do you accept Cigna insurance?`
2. Show that the chatbot gives a clear, accurate answer pulled from the business document.
3. Ask a question that is not covered in the FAQ.
4. Show that the chatbot escalates professionally instead of making something up.
