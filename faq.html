Getting Started

The practical questions people ask before booking or reserving.

Do I need to know anything about technology?

No. We handle the setup, training, testing, and maintenance. You tell us about your business, your services, and what is eating your time. We build the system around that.

How fast can you set it up?

Most Starter and Pro builds are live in 5 to 7 business days after kickoff. Bigger builds can take longer, but we confirm the exact scope and timing before we begin.

What happens after I reserve or buy?

You get confirmation right away. Trendell follows up within 24 hours to confirm fit, gather the right business details, and schedule your kickoff so the build can start cleanly.

Do you only work with Detroit businesses?

No. Genesis AI Systems is built in Detroit and serves businesses nationwide. The Detroit part is where we are from, not a limit on who we work with.

What kinds of businesses are the best fit?

Service businesses that lose time to missed calls, repetitive questions, lead follow-up, or appointment scheduling are the strongest fit. Restaurants, HVAC, dental, salons, and real estate are the most obvious examples, but the model applies more broadly.

Pricing & Billing

How deposits, plans, and checkout decisions work.

What does the $100 deposit do?

The $100 deposit reserves your build slot and kickoff time. We use the kickoff call to confirm the best-fit plan and apply that deposit toward the project. It is meant to create momentum without forcing you into the wrong package.

Why does Pro still route to a call instead of online checkout?

Pro usually needs a little more scoping before we lock the build. The call makes sure the follow-up logic, notifications, and reporting match how your business already operates.

Which plan should I start with?

Starter is best when you want one clear automation live fast. Pro is better when you need lead capture, scoring, and follow-up working together. Growth is for businesses that want the phone system and broader automation stack. Full Stack is the fully managed all-in build.

Can I change plans later?

Yes. Most clients start smaller, prove the value, and then add more systems. We can expand from Starter to Pro or Growth without rebuilding everything from scratch.

Are setup fees refundable?

Setup fees cover build work, configuration, testing, and rollout, so they are generally not refundable once the build has started. If we have not kicked off yet and something changed, talk to us early and we will work through it with you.

Support & Operations

What it is like once your system is live.

Will the AI sound like my business?

Yes. We train it on your business details, pricing, policies, tone, and common questions so it answers in a way that feels aligned with your business instead of generic.

What if something breaks?

We monitor the systems and handle issues when they come up. You are not expected to debug tools, API changes, or automation failures yourself. That ongoing support is part of what you are paying for.

Can the AI work with the tools I already use?

Often yes. It depends on what you are using and what outcome you want, but we can usually work with common tools or create a lighter process that keeps your team from needing more software than necessary.

Can I cancel?

Yes. Monthly services can be canceled with 30 days written notice. We do not lock you into long-term contracts just to keep a client on the books.

How do I know what was happening while I was busy?

That depends on the system, but usually you get some combination of text alerts, summaries, booking confirmations, lead logs, or scheduled reports so you are not guessing what happened after hours.

Still not sure?

If your situation is more specific than a FAQ answer, the fastest path is still a short call. We can tell you what we would build, what plan fits, and whether you should start smaller.
