# Case Studies — Genesis AI Systems

These are realistic before-and-after stories built around Smile Dental and other demo businesses. They show what each system does for a real owner.

## Case Study 1 — Never Miss a Customer Again

- Client type and size: Independent dental office, 2 locations, 8 staff
- Problem: 8 to 10 website leads per week sat too long. Response time was about 4 hours.
- Solution: We built a lead system that reads each inquiry, sorts urgency, saves it, and replies fast.
- How it works:
  - New inquiry comes in
  - System sorts it right away
  - Office gets the hot ones first
- Results:
  - Response time dropped from 4 hours to under 10 seconds
  - Estimated 2 to 4 extra appointments booked each week
  - Front desk saved 3 to 5 hours each week
- Tech stack used: n8n, OpenAI, Google Sheets, Gmail
- Timeline to deliver: 2 to 3 business days
- Investment: $500 setup + $150/month
- Client quote: "We finally knew which leads needed attention first instead of treating every inquiry the same."
